Photographer | Videomaker | Designer

Hello and welcome to my personal webpage! I am Jan (Honza) Levínský, a 17-year-old student at SPŠST Panská in Prague, Czech Republic, specializing in film and TV creation. Within my studies, I demonstrate excellence in technical and vocational subjects pertinent to my field of study, encompassing photography, studio technique, English, and art history. Speaking of art history, I have a profound fondness for artistic expression, particularly favoring the styles of Impressionism and Expressionism. Additionally, I have an interest in creating traditional art forms, although it's an area where I'm still developing my skills and expertise. Proficiency in English is a strong suit of mine, as I speak it fluently (almost), alongside Czech, which I speak perfectly as my native language. If you wish to get in touch, rest assured that I am more than capable of effectively communicating in both languages.
